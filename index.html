Pioneering Rapid In Vivo Screening
through C. elegans Model.

WormEra Research Lab is a specialized research and service laboratory focused on Caenorhabditis elegans–based in vivo screening for nutraceutical, pharmaceutical, antimicrobial and functional ingredient evaluation. With a strong emphasis on scientific accuracy, innovation and translational research, the laboratory provides reliable preclinical screening solutions for academia and industry.

Why WormEra

The Platform Advantage

C. elegans offers a unique combination of biological relevance, speed, and cost-efficiency.

Transparent organism

Enables live imaging, photography, and real-time monitoring at every stage of the assay.

Rapid lifecycle model

Short life cycle enables accelerated experimental timelines compared to rodent models.

Conserved biology

~1mm organism with conserved pathways relevant to human systems — true in vivo context beyond cell culture.

Cost-efficient screening

Organism-level screening with measurable phenotypic outputs at a fraction of mammalian study costs.
