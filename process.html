Submission Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to ensure your compound arrives in optimal condition for testing.

Compound Form

Powder preferred (min. 10 mg); DMSO solutions accepted (max 1% final concentration in assay).

Labeling Requirements

Compound name, molecular weight, CAS number, concentration (if solution), and safety hazard class.

Shipping Conditions

Ambient (stable compounds); 4°C (labile compounds). Include MSDS/SDS with each shipment.
