Why Train with WormEra

Become the C. elegans expert at your institution

Workshops at WormEra are designed for anyone looking to build genuine in-house C. elegans capability — from first exposure to advanced technique mastery. We position you as the authority, not just the client.

Our training programs are run by the same scientists who conduct our research — you're learning from published C. elegans researchers with 5+ years of hands-on expertise. Every workshop is backed by real data, real protocols, and real compound examples from our published studies.

Certificate of completion issued upon successful training
What You'll Learn

Training modules

Hands-on worm handling

Live culture maintenance, transfer techniques, synchronization, and strain management.

Assay setup & execution

End-to-end assay preparation, compound dosing, and real-time monitoring using live imaging.

Data analysis & interpretation

Statistical scoring, phenotypic interpretation, and presentation of publication-quality results.

Study design principles

Controls, replicates, blinding, and reproducibility — designing experiments that hold up to peer review.

Curriculum

Sample workshop curriculum

The curriculum is adapted based on participant background and workshop format. The following modules are covered in the certification program.

Module 01
Introduction to C. elegans Biology
Organism biology, life cycle, and genetics
Lab infrastructure and culture setup
Strain sourcing and maintenance
Ethics and advantages over mammalian models
Module 02
Basic Techniques & Handling
Synchronization and staging protocols
Compound preparation and dosing
Microscopy and live imaging basics
Documentation and photography
Module 03
Toxicity Assays
Acute lethality and LC50 determination
Developmental and reproductive toxicity
Behavioral assays (thrashing, pumping)
Data collection templates and scoring
Module 04
Efficacy & Functional Assays
Lifespan and healthspan assay setup
Stress resistance protocols
Probiotic and prebiotic screening
Anthelmintic activity determination
Module 05
Infection Models
Bacterial and fungal pathogen preparation
Infection assay protocols
Survival curve analysis
Anti-virulence compound screening
Module 06
Data Analysis & Reporting
Statistical tools and software overview
Graph preparation and phenotypic scoring
Interpreting and communicating results
Writing assay reports and regulatory summaries
