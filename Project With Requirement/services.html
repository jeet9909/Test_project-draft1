Services

What We Offer

Three core service pillars, each designed around your compound, your question, and your timeline. All studies use C. elegans as a whole-organism, in vivo model.

Toxicity & Safety Profiling

Whole-organism safety studies using C. elegans to generate reliable pre-clinical toxicity data for regulatory submissions, ingredient safety assessments, and formulation decision-making. Suitable for pharma, cosmetics, nutraceuticals, and agrochemical clients.

Acute Toxicity Testing
LC50 and EC50 determination with lethality, survival, and motility endpoints across multiple concentration ranges. Rapid turnaround for initial screening.
PharmaCosmeticsAgrochemicals
Sub-lethal & Chronic Toxicity
Developmental, reproductive, and behavioral endpoints over extended exposures at sub-lethal concentrations. Identifies long-term safety concerns.
PharmaNutraceuticals
Developmental & Reproductive Toxicity
Embryogenesis, larval development progression, brood size analysis, and reproductive output metrics for comprehensive developmental safety profiling.
AYUSHCosmeticsPharma
Behavioral & Locomotion Assays
Thrashing frequency, pharyngeal pumping rate, chemotaxis index, and body bending assays to detect neurotoxic and motor effects of test compounds.

Efficacy & Functional Bioassays

Validate the biological activity of your compound across a wide range of functional endpoints. From anti-aging and stress protection to probiotic screening and anthelmintic validation — powered by the C. elegans whole-organism model.

Healthspan & Lifespan Studies
Mean lifespan extension, age-related phenotypic improvements, and health index measurements. Used to validate longevity and healthspan claims for nutraceuticals and AYUSH products.
NutraceuticalsAYUSHCosmetics
Anti-aging & Stress Resistance

Microbiological & Anti-infective Assays

Host-pathogen interaction studies, antimicrobial resistance research, and anti-virulence compound screening — all conducted in a true in vivo infection model using C. elegans as host organism.

In vivo Infection Models
C. elegans infection with bacterial and fungal pathogens (Pseudomonas, Staphylococcus, Candida, Serratia) for validation of compound anti-infective activity in a living host.
PharmaBiotechnology
Anti-virulence Screening
