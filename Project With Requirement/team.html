The Team

Scientific expertise meets
business acumen

WormEra is led by two researchers from Nirma University with a combined track record of 15+ peer-reviewed publications and 5+ years of hands-on C. elegans expertise.

GG
Dr. Gemini Gajera
Co-Founder & Principal Scientist
Researcher, Nirma University, Ahmedabad

Dr. Gemini Gajera is a researcher at Nirma University with deep expertise in C. elegans biology, host-pathogen interactions, antimicrobial research, and validated experimental design. She has led multiple published studies in high-impact, peer-reviewed journals — contributing original research on nano-silver formulations, polyherbal anti-infectives, anti-virulence compounds, and C. elegans model development.

At WormEra, Dr. Gajera leads scientific strategy, study design, and research quality assurance — ensuring every assay meets the standards of publication-grade research.

Areas of Expertise
C. elegans Biology Host-Pathogen Interactions Antimicrobial Research Study Design Anti-virulence Screening Nano-silver Formulations

NT
Ms. Nidhi Thakkar
Co-Founder & Research Lead
PhD Thesis Submitted, Nirma University, Ahmedabad

Ms. Nidhi Thakkar has submitted her PhD thesis from Nirma University and specializes in stress biology, functional screening, and translational research. Her work spans fermentation science, microbiome studies, probiotic evaluation, and functional ingredient validation using C. elegans as the primary model organism.

At WormEra, Ms. Thakkar leads client relations, operational management, and research execution — bridging scientific rigor with the practical demands of industry clients across pharma, nutraceuticals, and AYUSH.
