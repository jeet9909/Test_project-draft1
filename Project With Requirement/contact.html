FAQ

Common questions

How long does a typical assay take?
Most standard assays are completed within 1–3 weeks from the date of sample receipt, depending on the type and complexity. Acute toxicity studies can often be turned around in 7–10 days.
What do I need to provide to get started?
Just your compound (a small quantity is sufficient), your target endpoint, and any regulatory or industry context. We'll design the study from there. A free consultation is always the first step.
Is C. elegans data accepted for regulatory submissions?
Yes — C. elegans data is increasingly accepted in pre-clinical submissions for nutraceuticals, cosmetics, and AYUSH products. For pharma, it serves as a powerful early-screening and complementary data source before mammalian studies.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes, we welcome international collaborations. Clients can ship compounds to our lab in Ahmedabad. We coordinate sample handling protocols and provide detailed interim and final reports digitally.
Can WormEra handle multiple compounds?
Absolutely. We offer subscription packages for multi-compound screening, ideal for R&D teams who need regular throughput. Contact us for a custom quote based on your pipeline.
How is data delivered?
All data is delivered in a structured report including raw data, statistical analysis, graphical outputs, and interpretation. Reports are designed to be directly usable for regulatory dossiers or internal decision-making.
