Our Story

Science without shortcuts

WormEra Research Lab is a specialized contract research organization (CRO) founded by researchers, for researchers. Built on over five years of hands-on expertise in Caenorhabditis elegans biology, we offer scientifically rigorous, whole-organism bioassay services to industries that demand reliable pre-clinical data — faster and more affordably than conventional models allow.

We are established in Ahmedabad and founded by researchers from Nirma University with deep expertise in host-pathogen interactions, stress biology, functional screening, and validated experimental design. With 15+ peer-reviewed publications and a commitment to reproducibility, our work is grounded in science — not shortcuts.

Because good science shouldn't be slow, expensive, or inaccessible. We built this lab to close the gap between promising compounds and credible biological evidence — with integrity, precision, and impact at every step.

Our Platform

Why C. elegans?

At the heart of WormEra is C. elegans — a ~1mm transparent nematode with conserved biological pathways remarkably relevant to human physiology. This whole-organism model enables us to go beyond conventional cell-based assays, delivering true in vivo context across toxicity, efficacy, and antimicrobial studies.

Transparent bodyEnables live imaging and real-time monitoring at every assay stage
Rapid lifecycleFaster results than rodent models, enabling quicker decision-making
Well-mapped geneticsEnables mechanistic insight into compound activity and toxicity
Cost-efficientOrganism-level data at a fraction of mammalian study costs
Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision
Bridge science and industry
To advance biological research through innovative and scientifically validated model systems that contribute to better health, sustainable science, and next-generation product development. We aim to bridge academic excellence with industrial research by delivering reliable and impactful scientific solutions globally.

To establish C. elegans as a trusted platform for early-stage decision-making in life sciences, and to make India a recognized hub for this emerging research discipline.
Mission
Rigorous science, accessible
Innovation, scientific integrity, quality, and collaboration are the core values of WormEra Research Lab. We are committed to providing standardized, accurate, and research-driven assay services using advanced in vivo model systems and modern scientific methodologies.

Our mission is to support researchers and industries with reliable biological screening platforms that accelerate scientific discovery, product validation, and translational research.
